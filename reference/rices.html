Rices!

White Rice

  • Rinse well. Add water to 1 fingernail height over rice and season.
  • Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
  • Let stand 10 minutes and fluff.

Wild Rice Mix

  • 2 cups rice : 3¼ cups water
  • Mix, season, cover and bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer 45-50 minutes.
  • Let stand 10 minutes and fluff.
